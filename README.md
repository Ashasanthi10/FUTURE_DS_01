#Online Retail Dashboard – README
##Project Title
##Online Retail Sales Analysis Dashboard
##Project Overview
This project analyzes online retail sales data to identify revenue trends, customer behavior, product performance, and regional sales distribution. The dashboard provides actionable insights for business decision-making through interactive visualizations and key performance indicators (KPIs).
##Objective
•	Analyze sales performance over time. 
•	Identify top-selling products and customers. 
•	Evaluate customer purchasing patterns. 
•	Compare revenue across countries/regions. 
•	Support data-driven business decisions. 
##Dataset
###Dataset: Online Retail Dataset
The dataset contains transactional records from a UK-based online retail store.
##Key Columns
Column Name	Description
InvoiceNo	Unique invoice number
StockCode	Product code
Description	Product description
Quantity	Quantity sold
InvoiceDate	Transaction date
UnitPrice	Price per unit
CustomerID	Customer identifier
Country	Customer country

##Dashboard KPIs
•	Total Revenue 
•	Total Orders 
•	Total Customers 
•	Average Order Value 
•	Average Unit Price 
##Dashboard Visuals
1. Revenue Over Time
•	Monthly revenue trend analysis. 
•	Identifies growth and seasonal patterns. 
2. Revenue by Country
•	Revenue contribution by different countries. 
•	Highlights top-performing regions. 
3. Geographic Revenue Map
•	Interactive map showing country-wise revenue. 
4. Top 10 Products by Revenue
•	Best-selling products based on sales revenue. 
5. Top 10 Customers by Revenue
•	Customers generating the highest revenue. 
6. New vs Repeat Customers
•	Customer retention and loyalty analysis. 
##Key Insights
1.	Revenue shows a consistent upward trend. 
2.	United Kingdom contributes the highest percentage of total sales. 
3.	A small group of products generates a significant portion of revenue. 
4.	Repeat customers contribute a major share of total revenue. 
5.	High-value customers significantly impact overall business performance. 
##Business Recommendations
•	Focus marketing campaigns on top-performing products. 
•	Improve customer retention programs. 
•	Expand operations in high-revenue countries. 
•	Offer loyalty rewards for repeat customers. 
•	Optimize inventory for fast-moving products. 
##Expected Outcomes
•	Better understanding of sales performance. 
•	Improved customer engagement strategies. 
•	Enhanced inventory planning. 
•	Increased profitability through data-driven decisions. 
##Conclusion
The Online Retail Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales, customers, products, and regional performance. It enables stakeholders to monitor KPIs, identify trends, and make informed business decisions to drive growth and profitability.
